# Prototype Overview

## Overview

This prototype demonstrates **Project TRUST**, a product enhancement proposal for Vedantu's consumer learning platform.

Instead of redesigning the application, the prototype introduces lightweight trust experiences that improve users' understanding of how their educational data supports learning.

The prototype follows one complete student journey while preserving Vedantu's existing product experience.

---

## Design Objective

Increase **User Confidence** without increasing **Learning Friction**.

The prototype is based on three design principles:

- Smart Consent
- Just-in-Time Disclosure
- Progressive Transparency

---

## Primary Persona

**Student**

Age: 15

Grade: 10

Goal:

- Attend live classes quickly
- Improve academic performance
- Receive personalized learning recommendations

---

## User Journey

```
Dashboard
      ↓
Join Live Class
      ↓
Smart Camera Consent
      ↓
Attend Live Session
      ↓
AI Recommendation
      ↓
Why Am I Seeing This?
      ↓
Learning & Privacy
      ↓
Manage Learning Preferences
```

---

## Prototype Scope

The prototype intentionally focuses on one student journey.

Parent and Teacher experiences were considered during product discovery but are outside the scope of this demonstration to maintain clarity and avoid feature overload.

---

## Screens Included

### Screen 1
Student Dashboard

Purpose

Shows the existing Vedantu experience before introducing trust enhancements.

---

### Screen 2
Smart Camera Consent

Introduces contextual permission explanations without interrupting learning.

---

### Screen 3
Live Class

Adds contextual recording transparency.

---

### Screen 4
AI Recommendation

Explains why recommendations are generated.

---

### Screen 5
Learning & Privacy

Provides simple explanations about learning data instead of legal terminology.

---

### Screen 6
Manage Learning Preferences

Allows students to control personalization and educational data.

---

## Design Principles

✔ Research before redesign

✔ Learning first

✔ Invisible Trust

✔ Minimal Friction

✔ Privacy should answer questions

✔ Just-in-Time Disclosure

---

## Expected Product Outcomes

- Higher user confidence
- Better understanding of AI recommendations
- Increased transparency
- Improved trust
- No increase in onboarding friction

---

## Prototype Goal

This prototype demonstrates how trust can become part of the learning experience without redesigning Vedantu's existing product.
