# Prototype Screens

## Student Journey

### 1. Student Dashboard



The existing Vedantu dashboard remains unchanged. The trust experience begins only when it becomes contextually relevant.

---

### 2. Smart Camera Consent



Instead of requesting camera access without explanation, the interface briefly explains why the permission improves classroom interaction.

---

### 3. Live Class



A lightweight recording notice reassures students that recordings are used for revision and classroom safety.

---

### 4. AI Recommendation



Students can understand why recommendations appear through contextual explanations rather than hidden algorithms.

---

### 5. Learning & Privacy



Learning data is explained in simple language, focusing on educational value instead of legal terminology.

---

### 6. Manage Learning Preferences


Students can manage personalization and learning data while maintaining confidence and control.

## Prototype Goal

Demonstrate how contextual trust can be embedded into Vedantu's existing student journey without increasing friction.

The prototype is intentionally lightweight and focuses on validating the product strategy rather than redesigning the application.

## Core Design Principles

- Privacy should answer questions, not ask more of the user.
- Just-in-Time Disclosure.
- Progressive Transparency.
- Research before redesign.
- Learning first.
