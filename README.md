# vedantu-product-trust-case-study
# Beyond Compliance
## Designing Invisible Trust into Vedantu's Learning Experience

> A Product Management case study exploring how an established EdTech platform can transform privacy from a compliance requirement into a trust-building product experience.

---

## Project Overview

This repository contains my end-to-end Product Management case study completed around Vedantu's consumer product ecosystem.

Rather than redesigning the platform, this project identifies an opportunity to improve how students experience privacy and trust while preserving Vedantu's fast, personalized learning journey.

The proposal introduces **Project TRUST**, an experience layer built around **Smart Consent**, **Just-in-Time Disclosure**, and **Progressive Transparency**.

---

## The Product Challenge

Vedantu already demonstrates strong privacy practices, child safety mechanisms, and regulatory alignment.

The opportunity is **not improving compliance**.

The opportunity is improving **how users experience privacy**.

### Core Insight

> Users are protected.

> But users rarely experience those protections.

This creates an **Experience Gap** rather than a **Compliance Gap**.

# Product Philosophy

> Privacy should answer questions, not ask more of the user.

Design Principles

- Research before redesign
- Learning first
- Just-in-Time Disclosure
- Progressive Transparency
- Invisible Trust
- Minimal Friction

---

# Proposed Solution

The proposal introduces **Project TRUST**, an invisible Trust Layer integrated into Vedantu's existing product experience.

Instead of adding new privacy workflows, the solution enhances existing trust moments throughout the student journey.

Core capabilities include:

- Smart Consent
- Just-in-Time Disclosure
- Learning & Privacy
- Explainable AI Recommendations
- Contextual Recording Transparency
- Lightweight Learning Data Controls
